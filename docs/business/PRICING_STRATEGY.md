## Frappe Cloud Marketplace

On Frappe Cloud, premium features are auto-enabled based on subscription plan.
No license key needed — detected automatically.

## Self-Hosted Licensing

For self-hosted installations:
1. Purchase a license key from Arkan Lab
2. Enter the key in **AuraCRM Settings > License & Subscription**
3. Premium features activate immediately

License keys are per-site and validated locally (no phone-home).
