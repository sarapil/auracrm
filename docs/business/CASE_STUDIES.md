## Case Study Ideas (Pre-Launch)

### 1. Real Estate Agency
- **Scenario**: Property developer managing 500+ leads/month
- **AuraCRM features**: Lead scoring by budget/location, property inventory, deal rooms, sold-proof social posts
- **Expected outcomes**: 40% faster lead response, 25% higher conversion

### 2. B2B SaaS Company
- **Scenario**: SaaS startup with 3-person sales team
- **AuraCRM features**: Pipeline board, gamification, campaign sequences, AI profiler
- **Expected outcomes**: Gamification increases team engagement by 60%

### 3. Marketing Agency
- **Scenario**: Agency managing campaigns for 10+ clients
- **AuraCRM features**: Marketing lists, social publishing, content calendar, competitive intel
- **Expected outcomes**: Centralized campaign management saves 15 hours/week

### 4. Arabic-First Enterprise
- **Scenario**: GCC company needing full Arabic CRM
- **AuraCRM features**: Full RTL, 1,539 Arabic translations, holiday guard for regional calendars
- **Expected outcomes**: First fully Arabic CRM on ERPNext ecosystem
