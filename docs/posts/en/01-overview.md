## Why AuraCRM?

Businesses in the Customer Relations space face real challenges:
- ❌ Salesforce is too expensive for SMBs
- ❌ CRM data disconnected from ERP/accounting
- ❌ Sales team motivation and tracking
- ❌ Manual data entry for contact information
- ❌ No unified customer view across departments

**AuraCRM solves all of this** — natively inside Frappe/ERPNext.
