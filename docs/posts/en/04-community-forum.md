# [Announcement] AuraCRM — Smart CRM with AI Insights for ERPNext | Open Source

Hi Frappe Community! 👋

We're excited to share **AuraCRM**, a new open-source customer relations app for Frappe/ERPNext.

## What it does

✅ AI-Powered Lead Scoring
✅ OSINT Contact Enrichment
✅ Sales Gamification & Leaderboards
✅ Visual Sales Pipeline (Kanban)
✅ Email & Call Tracking
✅ Territory Management
✅ Customer 360° Dashboard
✅ Predictive Analytics
✅ Social Media Integration
✅ Custom Report Builder

## Why we built it

- Salesforce is too expensive for SMBs
- CRM data disconnected from ERP/accounting
- Sales team motivation and tracking
- Manual data entry for contact information
- No unified customer view across departments

We couldn't find a good customer relations solution that integrates natively with ERPNext, so we built one.

## Tech Stack

- **Backend:** Python, Frappe Framework v16
- **Frontend:** JavaScript, Frappe UI, frappe_visual components
- **Database:** MariaDB (standard Frappe)
- **License:** MIT
- **Dependencies:** frappe_visual, caps, arkan_help

## Installation

```bash
bench get-app https://github.com/sarapil/auracrm
bench --site your-site install-app auracrm
bench --site your-site migrate
```
